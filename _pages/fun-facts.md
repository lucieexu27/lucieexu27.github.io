## Running

![Running with fellow Atlanta Track Club ambassadors.](/images/running2.jpg)

![First marathon in the books!](/images/running2.jpeg)

Running has become a big part of my life in Atlanta. I've connected with the local running community through the Atlanta Track Club as an ambassador, and have had a lot of fun at the various races through the year. I recently ran my first full marathon and got to explore the Silver Comet Trail while doing so!

## Music

![Chamber music in college.](/images/quintet.jpg)

![Soundchecking for Yo-Yo Ma at the Georgetown SFS Centennial Gala.](/images/yoyoma.JPG)

I play the piano and cello for fun and have been lucky to share music with others over the years — from touring with an orchestra in Portugal to performing at Carnegie Hall. In college, I also loved playing chamber music, including a memorable double-cello quintet. I also had the chance to soundcheck for Yo-Yo Ma and the Silk Road Ensemble at a gala!
